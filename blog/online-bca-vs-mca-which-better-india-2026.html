Online BCA vs MCA India 2026 — Which Degree Should You Choose?

BCA vs MCA — the key difference

BCA is a 3-year undergraduate degree. MCA is a 2-year postgraduate degree. They are not alternatives — they serve different career stages. No CS UG degree → BCA first. Have BCA/B.Sc CS → MCA is the upgrade.

Best path in 2026

The highest-ROI path: Online BCA → AI/DS upskilling (IIT certificate or NPTEL) → developer role.
