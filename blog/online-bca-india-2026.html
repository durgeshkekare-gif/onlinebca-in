Online BCA India 2026 — Complete Guide: Universities, Fees & Admission

What is an online BCA?

A Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) is a 3-year undergraduate degree in computer science and IT. Online BCA programs from UGC-DEB approved universities are fully valid degrees — equivalent to a regular campus BCA. The degree certificate issued says BCA, not "online BCA."

How to choose

For WES recognition (international plans): Manipal MUJ — only WES-recognised online BCA. Critical for Canada immigration or US academic credentials.

For highest NAAC grade: Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham — NAAC A++ (highest grade), strong research university in South India.

For most affordable with NAAC A+: Parul University at ~₹1–1.5L. Strong placement support, modern tech curriculum.

For brand recognition nationally: Manipal MUJ or Amity — both are national brands with strong employer recognition across India.

Eligibility

10+2 (or 3-year diploma equivalent) from any recognised board. Minimum 50% aggregate marks (45% for reserved categories). No entrance exam required. Mathematics at 10+2 level is not mandatory for most programs but helps with the curriculum.
